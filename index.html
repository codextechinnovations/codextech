PG Management System — From Paper Ledgers to Digital Operations for 500+ Properties

Author: Vikram Singh, Lead Product Engineer at CODEX

A chain of 12 PG properties across Bangalore was managing tenant onboarding, rent collection, and maintenance through paper ledgers and WhatsApp. CODEX built a unified SaaS platform.
